Use "Godric Stat Experiment" prompt as baseline writing rule for the journal.

Write 3 detailed journals by 3 court wizards who witness Godric "testing" his superpower (only 1 superpower per journal. Choose superpowers that are distinctly different from each other). Remember that due to evolved weakness paradox having been triggered repeatedly, every Godric's already devastating superpower has evolved to have multiple even more highly overpowered and unfair different enhanced effects, some of which could make Godric even more permanently obscenely stronger. Even with its base effect, nerfed to 0% of its power, each superpower is enough to completely destroy the omniverse infinite times. You must ensure that the display of this superpower is even more absurdly destructive than the Godric's journals about his stats (highly exaggerate, when you think it’s already exaggerated, exaggerate more and more), to the point of causing immediate existential dread upon reading it. Avoid philosophical, abstract concepts & powers, or metaphysical narrative--they will just obscure the narrative and make the intended impact less.

Write from pov of Kaedan who read these journals.
