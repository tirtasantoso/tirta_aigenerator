**Year 310,204 — Elias — Endurance**

Clive sat in the silence of his quarters and stared at the wall and felt the dissonance settle into his bones like a second skeleton, a framework of absolute existential horror that would support him for the remaining years of his mortal life, and the skeleton was made of the same material as the marble, and the marble was made of the same material as existence, and existence was made of the same material as nothing, and nothing was what Godric had expressed when he had accidentally annihilated everything, and the nothing was sufficient, and the sufficiency was absolute, and the absoluteness was the nature of Godric's power, and the nature was such that opposition was impossible, and the impossibility was eternal, and the eternity was guaranteed, and the guarantee was the marble on the desk, and the marble was the trophy, and the trophy was the truth, and the truth was absolute, and the absoluteness was Godric, and Godric was.

And there would never be another opposition.

Ever.
