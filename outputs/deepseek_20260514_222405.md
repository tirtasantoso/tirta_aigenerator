And I am terrified beyond the concept of terror. Because I now understand that the apex predator is not the top of the chain. The chain is infinite, and every link is Him, and every link is infinitely, unspeakably beyond the one before. And I, Zeus, descendant of Blake, am a mote of dust in the shadow of the first link, grateful to exist, grateful to serve, and absolutely, utterly, eternally afraid.
