But I was intimately, horrifyingly aware that we were nothing more than microscopic bacteria living in the shadow of a biology that could casually erase creation with a fever. The infinite power gap between Godric and the rest of existence was an abyss that stared back, and all I could do was wait in paralyzed dread for the next time the god decided he wanted to play.
