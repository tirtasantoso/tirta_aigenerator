Based on the text provided, **Godric would win against Superman**—and it wouldn’t even be a contest.

While Superman is one of the most powerful icons in fiction, the version of Godric described in this file is a "Tier-0" or "Boundless" entity designed specifically to be a narrative singularity that cannot be defeated.

Here is a detailed breakdown of why Godric wins based on the specific mechanics mentioned in the document:

### 1. The Math of Infinite Growth
Superman’s power is generally finite (though vast), often tied to the amount of yellow solar radiation he has absorbed. Godric’s power, however, is a **geometric progression** that has been running for **110,000 years**.
*   **The Document states:** Godric’s strength, speed, and senses multiply by one million, and then by the resulting sum, *every single second*. 
*   **The Result:** After even one hour of this, Godric’s physical stats would exceed the total number of atoms in the multiverse. After 110,000 years, his power is mathematically incomprehensible. He doesn’t just have "super strength"; he possesses the power to shatter "celestial spheres" with a literal exhale.

### 2. The "Absolute Defense" (Anti-Strategy)
This is the specific mechanic that makes Superman’s primary advantage—his tactical mind and morality—useless. 
*   **The Document states:** If any entity even *thinks* that Godric might have a flaw or a weakness, that thought triggers an immediate spike in Godric’s power. Godric then gains "absolute mastery" over the very concept the enemy hoped to use.
*   **The Battle Scenario:** If Superman tries to use Godric's grief for his family against him, or tries to find a "Kryptonite" equivalent for Godric, Godric would instantly become immune to that concept and gain total control over it. He would "own" Superman’s logic and strategy before Clark could even throw a punch.

### 3. Erasure of Concepts
Superman relies on the laws of physics (gravity, light, force) to operate. Godric can rewrite these laws at will.
*   **The Document states:** Godric can "collapse the molecular bonds of an entire solar system with a thought" and "rewrite the laws of causality across a galaxy."
*   **The Feat:** Godric defeated a "Weaver of Fates"—a being that controls destiny—by making it forget the "concept of action." He could simply remove Superman’s ability to understand how to fly or how to use his heat vision, or remove the concept of "The Sun" from reality entirely.

### 4. Comparison of Scale
*   **Superman's Limit:** Even in his strongest incarnations (like *Strange Visitor* or *Cosmic Armor Superman*), Superman is a defender of the multiverse who operates within the "Story."
*   **Godric's Limit:** Godric is described as a being whose "sheer weight of boredom" is enough to subjugate a billion stars. He doesn't need to fight; his passive "Absolute Veil" is the only thing preventing his mere presence from erasing all of reality.

### 5. Speed and Perception
Superman can move faster than light, but Godric’s mind operates across "the gaps between seconds."
*   **The Document states:** Godric is omniscient. He sees the entire history of the galaxy "like a stained map." He would see Superman coming from a different universe before Superman even decided to travel there.

### Conclusion
Superman’s greatest strength is his willpower and his ability to find a way to win against impossible odds. However, **Godric’s power specifically punishes the act of trying to find a way to win.** 

In a fight, Superman would find that his strength meant nothing against a man who multiplies his power by trillions every second. Every time Superman tried a new tactic (magic, solar flares, speed blitzing), Godric would instantly become the master of that tactic. Godric wouldn't even need to stand up from his throne; he could simply "deny Superman's right to have ever existed as a threat," much like he did to the Weaver of Fates.
