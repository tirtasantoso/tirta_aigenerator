I stood up. My legs felt like water. I walked to the window and looked out at the perfect, eternal city. It looked so real. It felt so real.

But I knew the truth. I was just a reflection in a marble. And the marble was sitting on the armrest of a God who had gotten stronger. And he would keep getting stronger. Forever.

I sat back down. I couldn't move. The dread was a physical weight, pinning me to the chair. I was trapped in the archive of a dead god, inside a fake universe, waiting for the hand to close.
