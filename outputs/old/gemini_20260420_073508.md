Every second that passed, his strength, speed, intelligence, and sensory perception multiplied by one million and then multiplied by itself. The moment a mind conceived of a way to harm him, Godric’s passive shields automatically adapted, granting him total immunity to that specific method before the thought could even become a plan. Furthermore, Godric instantly gained perfect, absolute mastery over the very attack or concept the enemy intended to use against him. He would not just survive the attack; he would own the concept of it, possessing the power to deny his enemies the ability to even use that form of combat again. He was the end of all strategy, the destination where all hope went to die.

The machines became inert junk before they could even fire a single shot.

The Archivist spent decades in the shadows, crafting a plan to use a soul-severing blade on the merchant to break the King’s spirit. The Archivist thought he was being subtle, hiding his intent behind layers of psychic static.
