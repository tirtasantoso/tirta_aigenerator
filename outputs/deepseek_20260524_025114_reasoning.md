**Year 200,000 — The Incident of Speed — Account by Veren, Seventh Court Wizard of Thronegate**

**Year 750,000 — The Incident of Senses — Account by Theron, Twelfth Court Wizard of Thronegate**

**Year 400,000 — The Incident of Luck — Account by Marius, Tenth Court Wizard of Thronegate**

**Year 900,000 — The Incident of Wisdom — Account by Darius, Fifteenth Court Wizard of Thronegate**
